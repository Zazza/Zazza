### 💡 Engineering Philosophy
- Architecture > Syntax: I document why, not just how.
- AI as a multiplier: I use LLMs for boilerplate & research, but keep design, error handling, and UX in human hands.
- Build for humans: Lower the barrier to entry without sacrificing power users' control.
- Ship, measure, iterate: Prefer working software over perfect abstractions.
